Find out how to run your flash games.

1. Go to The Adobe Website in order to download the Flash Player projector, (which you will use to play your games).

2. Download the Flash player projector for your operating system.
3. Run the Exe file you just downloaded.
4. Then when the program has opened, press "File" in the top left hand corner.
5. Click on "Open".
6. Click on "Browse".
7. Navigate to the ".SWF" file that you just downloaded from our website, (it should be in your downloads folder).
8. Left click on the SWF file and then press "Open".
9. Then click OK.
10. Enjoy your game!
